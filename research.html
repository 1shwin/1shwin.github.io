Computational modelling of interactions between Bromodomains in BRD4 protein and Natural Product Ligands

Links: Report, PPT

Collaborators: Ananya Narayanan, Dr. V. Subramanian

Abstract: BRD4 (Bromodomain-containing protein 4) is a key epigenetic modulator of histones that plays a role in several disease pathways, including basal breast cancer, NUT midline carcinoma and HIV, among others. MGLTools and AutoDock Vina were used to simulate the docking of 5 natural-product based ligands (namely aristoyagonine, buxifoliadine-D, candidine, palodesangren-B and PCG) on the BRD4 protein. LigPlot+ was used to analyse the structures of the protein-ligand complexes.
